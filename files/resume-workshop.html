Overall Tips

  • No sentences or periods
  • Use correct verb tense
  • Don't use personal pronouns (I, we, us)
  • Eliminate fillers (a, an, the)
  • Check for misspellings & grammatical errors
  • Avoid using software templates
  • 2–6 bullets per experience (with exceptions!)
  • Don't include personal information (like your SSN# or birthdate) or photos

PSU 6 Assignment – Part 1

SmealConnect (2 points)

SmealConnect is a Smeal College of Business online database system designed to connect students with recruiters for internship and co-op opportunities.

  • Go to careerconnections.smeal.psu.edu/internships-co-ops/smeal-connect and review the instructions for gaining access to the SmealConnect system.
  • Read, sign, and submit the user agreement.
  • Watch the webshop on how to use the system.
  • Once you receive notice that your account has been opened, log into the system and update your profile.
  • Your instructor will receive a report of who has set up their account in the SmealConnect system.

PSU 6 Assignment – Part 2

Resume (8 points, including using VMock)

  • Review the resume webshop (6 minutes).
  • Review sample resumes.
  • Create a resume based on what you learned in the webshop and using the sample resumes as a guide. Save a copy of your resume in PDF format.
  • Create your account with VMock, an online resume evaluation tool. Follow the instructions on the instruction sheet — make sure you use the /smeal web address.
  • Upload your resume to VMock. VMock compares and scores your resume against other Smeal resumes.
  • Receive and review feedback from VMock, update your resume, and upload the second version. You should see improvement in your score.
  • You are only required to upload your resume one additional time, but you can upload it up to 10 times. You may also visit the Career & Corporate Connections Office in 114 Business Building for more help.

Submit the following to the dropbox in Canvas:

  • Your original resume and VMock score
  • Your final resume and VMock score
